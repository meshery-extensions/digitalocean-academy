The DO-CAIE assessment combines a **written exam** with a **hands-on capstone**. You must pass
both to earn the credential.

## Written exam structure

- **Format:** multiple-choice only - single-answer and multiple-answer questions.
- **Questions:** 60 scored questions, drawn from a larger pool.
- **Duration:** 90 minutes.
- **Pass mark:** 70%.
- **Delivery:** online, proctored, taken directly inside the academy.

## Domain blueprint

Every question maps to one of seven domains. The weighting tells you where to focus your study time.

| # | Domain | Weight |
|---|--------|--------|
| 1 | AI-Native Cloud fundamentals & service selection | 12% |
| 2 | Foundation models, serverless inference & the model catalog | 14% |
| 3 | Building agents with the Gradient AI Platform | 18% |
| 4 | Knowledge Bases & RAG | 14% |
| 5 | GPU compute, 1-Click Models & model serving | 14% |
| 6 | Cloud native AI operations with Meshery & DOKS | 16% |
| 7 | Production engineering: routing, security, observability & responsible AI | 12% |

Domains 3 and 6 carry the most weight: building real agents and operating the infrastructure that
serves them are the heart of the credential.

## Capstone

The capstone is a practical, end-to-end build (see the **Capstone Project Guide** course). It is
graded against a rubric covering functionality, grounding/RAG quality, infrastructure and Meshery
operations, safety (guardrails/evaluations), and documentation. A passing capstone plus a passing
written exam earns the credential.

## Scoring rules

- Each written question is worth a fixed number of marks; multiple-answer questions require **all**
  correct options and **no** incorrect ones for full marks.
- Every question is auto-graded against its correct answer(s).
- Your final written score is the percentage of total marks earned. **70%** or higher passes.
- The capstone is pass/fail against the rubric, with reviewer feedback.

## What to bring

For the written exam: nothing but focus — it is closed-book. For the capstone: a DigitalOcean
account (trial credit is fine), the `doctl` CLI, and a Meshery deployment.
